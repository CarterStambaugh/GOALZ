GOAL.Z

Released September 1, 2019

Shoot the ball into the goal by aiming and blasting your way past defenders, sharks, more!
Description
Shooting balls into goals have never been more fun than in GOALZ! Aim your way past a multitude of defenders and score goals to advance to new levels. As you get into higher and higher levels, you may find yourself discovering new worlds, each with their own distinct play-styles and obstacles. Carefully strategize how you can use the special items that are found during gameplay to score goals. With a precisely engineered aiming and collision system, you will find yourself truly immersed into the gameplay. Score as many goals as you can, and you will find surprises at every corner of the game!
